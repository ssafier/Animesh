How to use it:
Save the above code as convert_avsitter.php on your Mac.

Put your AVsitter notecard in the same folder and name it AVpos.txt.

Open your Mac Terminal, navigate to the folder, and run:

Bash
php convert_avsitter.php
It will generate animations.txt and menu.txt instantly.

-----
Save the above code as convert_mlp.php on your Mac.

Export your MLP notecards and save them in the same folder as the PHP script. Name them menuitems.txt and positions.txt.

Open your Mac Terminal, navigate to the folder, and run:

Bash
php convert_mlp.php
